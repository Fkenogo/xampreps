**Explanation:** After the decimal point, the first digit is tenths (0), the second is hundredths (7), and the third is thousandths (1). So 7 is in the hundredths place.

## Q6

## Q31

## Q43

**Explanation:** Every right angled triangle has one angle equal to 90°. The other two angles must add to 90°, so both are less than 90°. Therefore the largest angle is always 90°.
